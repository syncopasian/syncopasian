To commemorate Asian American and Pacific Islander Heritage month this May and celebrate all that the AAPI community has brought to the United States, Syncopasian has released a very special project years in the making. Our new music video Things We Carry on the Sea is an interpretation of Wang Ping's poem of the same name, conjuring essential facets of the immigrant experience across time and space. To help bring this project to life, eighteen speakers of various Asian/Pacific Islander languages have graciously lent words from their mother tongues, highlighting the rich cultural diversity of the various peoples celebrated this month.
